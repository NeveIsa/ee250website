###### 7. SSH from Ubuntu VM to Raspberry Pi (RPi)
- `ssh pi@raspberry`
- `ssh pi@raspberry.local`
- `ssh pi@AA.BB.CC.DD` where AA.BB.CC.DD is the IPv4 address of the RPi.
